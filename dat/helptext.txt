Your aim is to survive the longest you can while avoiding
obstacles created by game.
When orange rectangle appears on screen, you can jump
into it and begin to play match-3 game! If you manage
to collect some matches within 15 seconds, you
will get time of extra speed and being invisible
for obstacles - the more matches, the longer bonus!

Controls:
Space - jump (possible multiple jumps while uprising)
Mouse - moving tiles in match-3
